Engagement Phases

Phase A builds MVDS systems grounded in reality. Phase B prevents “frozen” tools and unlocks full operational impact via integration.

Phase A: Assessment & Activation (90 days)

  • Document collection + policy ingestion kickoff
  • Leadership + frontline interviews
  • Scenario mapping + evaluation alignment
  • Confirm what data/artifacts exist and in what format
  • Build and validate initial systems with weekly feedback cycles
Goal: Build and validate the initial systems using Zia’s existing policies, procedures, and available historical artifacts/data.

Phase B: Ongoing Activation & Integration (Annual Program)

Phase B keeps the system “alive” so it continues improving over time — with ongoing updates, continuous improvement, and most importantly EMR/workflow integration so intelligence is available in the flow of work.

  • Continuous ingestion/versioning of updated policies and training content
  • Continuous scenario expansion and rubric calibration
  • Operational dashboards with stable cadence + tuned alert thresholds
  • EMR/workflow integration + automated pipelines
  • Ongoing rollout support across locations and staffing changes
Without Phase B, Zia retains MVDS tools built in Phase A, but they won’t stay current as procedures evolve, won’t continuously refresh scenarios/analytics, and won’t reach full impact without workflow/EMR integration.

MVDS (Delivered within 90 days)

Annual Expansion (Phase B)

  • Continuous ingestion/versioning of updated policies + training content

Workstream C: Supervision & QA Intelligence Increase supervision leverage without increasing supervision hours

MVDS (Delivered within 90 days)
